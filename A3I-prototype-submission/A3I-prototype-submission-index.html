📋 How to Submit Your Application

Option 1: Online Submission

Visit the Tamil Nadu e-Governance Agency portal:

https://ssp24-25.tnega.org/institute/approve-list.html

Upload your filled application form and required documents.

Option 2: Offline Submission

1. Download and print your application form

2. Attach required documents (Aadhaar, income certificate, etc.)

3. Visit your local District Education Office

4. Submit the printed application with documents

Required Documents:

  • Aadhaar Card copy
  • Income Certificate
  • Community Certificate (if applicable)
  • Previous year mark sheets
  • Bank account details
  • Passport size photograph
