## 📋 **Testing Instructions for Judges**

### **Test Scholarship Flow:**
1. Open the prototype link
2. Select language (English/Hindi/Tamil)
3. Click "Get Started"
4. Enter: "I need scholarship for engineering"
5. Answer 10 eligibility questions
6. See AI-recommended schemes
7. Generate and review form
8. Submit and get confirmation

**Expected Time:** 3-5 minutes

### **Test Farmer Flow:**
1. Open the prototype link
2. Select language
3. Click "Get Started"
4. Enter: "I am a farmer looking for subsidy"
5. Select from 6 farmer schemes
6. Fill simple form (client-side)
7. Submit and get confirmation

**Expected Time:** 1-2 minutes
